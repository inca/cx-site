Docco: Quick start

Use With Existing Projects

If you already have a project and wish to use one of the Circumflex Docco component, just add the corresponding dependency to your project's pom.xml:

<properties>
  <cx.version><!-- desired version --></cx.version>
</properties>
<dependencies>
  <!-- Circumflex Docco -->
  <dependency>
    <groupId>ru.circumflex</groupId>
    <artifactId>circumflex-docco</artifactId>
    <version>{cx.version}</version>
  </dependency>
</dependencies>

The usage is trivial:

Docco("my.scala").toHtml("my.scala.html")

or shortcut of above:

Docco("my.scala").toHtml

Docco uses FreeMarker to process pages, so you can provide your own FreeMarker Configuration and templates.
